Rolls Royce Phantom

HATS OFF! TO YOUR CHOICE

INTERIORS:

If you had to describe Rolls Royce Phantom's interiors in one word, it would be “Vintage”. However these interiors are complimented with suavely designed hand crafted features and technical advancements alike. The rear hinged coach doors and deep roof pillar gives the rear passengers a sense of theater and privacy. The starlight headline along with 800 optic fibre is equipped in the car to make the ambiance like romantic night sky. Almost each and every element can be configured according to the need, taste and requirement of an individual. To enjoy the ride with good music the car is equipped with 13-speaker music system along with two bass speakers. The infotainment system with 8.8 inch-screen located in center makes the journey more entertaining and gives a clear view during navigation. The Rotary Controller is installed in the car that gives the overall control of the car on fingertips. The boot space of 460-litres in the car is sufficient for carrying enough baggage during long rides.
ENGINE SPECS:

The royal Phantom is customised across the globe under 4 design variants namely Phantom, Phantom Extended Wheelbase, Phantom Drophead Coupe and Phantom Coupe. The utmost luxury of the car is accompanied with powerful 12-cylinder 48 valves engine and direct fuel injection that gives the maximum power output measuring 460 PS at 5350 rpm and maximum torque of 720 Nm at 3500 rpm. The powerfull engine helps the driver in reaching 100 km/hr mark in 6.1 seconds and a top speed of 240 km/hr. The fuel economy is not impressive, as the car gives brings about a mileage of 14.1 L/100 kms.
EXTERIORS:


The designing of Rolls Royce Phantom is what makes it rather one of the most supremely elegant drive in the world today. The roof-line curve of Phantom gives a feeling of elegance and gives goosebumps even when the car is stationary. The dynamic features of the car are highlighted with chrome plating and brushed steel bonnet that clearly depicts the sleek designing of the car. The luxury of Rolls Royce Phantom embeds an overall dimension count measuring 5842 mm x 1990 mm x 1638 mm (L X W X H) alongside a wheelbase measuring 3570 mm that paves way for sufficient leg room, head room and shoulder room to the occupants. The 21-inch worth big alloy wheels of the car gives a road hugging experience to the driver with a tyre size of 255/ 50 R21.
WHEELS AND SUSPENSION SYSTEM :


The powerful stance, iconic proportions and immense luxury of car is accompanied with state of art technology that ensures the safety of the car in an effective manner. The ventilated discs are associated with Anti- Lock Braking System (ABS), Stability Control, Front, side, overhead and knee airbags for enhanced safety of the occupants. The Anti whiplash and pretensioners and security system installed in the car further enhances the security of Phantom.
