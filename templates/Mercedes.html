WHEELS AND SUSPENSION SYSTEM :


Wheels The sports car explores the roads running on the 19 inch alloy wheels and rear 20 inch wheels for the AMG GT S variant, with tyre dimensions reading 265/35 ZR 19 for front wheels and 295/30 ZR 20 for rear wheels. Braking and Handling The furious speeds of the fast sports car are restrained and kept well in controls by the well-calibrated handling assistance added to it. The retardation and handling assistance of the car is a function of the efficient braking system consisting of front and rear vented and cross-drilled discs brakes on front as well as rear wheels with handling assistance provided by the ABS (Antilock Braking System) and EBD (Electronic Brake-Force Distribution). The ultra-responsive car also encloses other handling assistance like the 3-stage Electronic Stability Program, hill start assist, adaptive braking system and Acceleration Skid Control(ASR), which makes the car's handling extremely safe and controllable. The car body is supported upon the double-wishbone type front and rear suspension.
